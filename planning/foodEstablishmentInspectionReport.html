OBSERVATIONS 25 DCMR CORRECTIVE ACTIONS
46. - There is no detergent-sanitizer available for use in the 2-compartment sinks. (CORRECT VIOLATION WITH 45 CALENDAR DAYS)  1601.4 Before a two (2)-compartment sink is used: (a) The licensee shall have its use approved; and (b) The nature of warewashing shall be limited to batch operations for cleaning kitchenware such as between cutting one type of raw meat and another, or cleanup at the end of a shift, and: (3) A detergent-sanitizer shall be used to sanitize and shall be applied as specified in section 1814.  
50. - The garbage disposal unit (food waste grinder) is directly connected to the drain at the bottom of one compartment of the 2-conmpartment sink. (CORRECT VIOLATION WITHIN 5 CALENDAR DAYS)  2602.1 Except as specified in sections 2602.2 and 2602.3, a direct connection shall not exist between the sewage system and a drain originating from equipment in which food, portable equipment, or utensils are placed.
